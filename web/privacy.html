Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

RulesAI is built by an individual developer. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and who we share it with.

Information We Collect

  • Account information — your name and email address, collected when you sign in via email or Google.
  • Usage data — the questions you ask and the governing body (e.g. Diamond Youth Baseball, NFHS Softball) you select. This is used to answer your queries and track your monthly usage limit.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active paid subscription, managed through RevenueCat.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and manage your account.
  • To answer your rules questions using our AI-powered lookup service.
  • To enforce the free tier query limit and manage your subscription.
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of the app over time.

Third-Party Services

RulesAI relies on the following third-party services to operate:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and usage data storage.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and in-app purchase processing.
  • Anthropic — AI language model that generates answers to your queries. Your questions are sent to Anthropic's API for processing.
  • Pinecone — vector database used to retrieve relevant rulebook content.
  • Voyage AI — embedding model used to match your questions to rulebook content.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Data Retention

Usage data is retained on a rolling monthly basis for the purpose of enforcing query limits. Account information is retained for as long as your account is active.

Security

All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted. We use Firebase's secure authentication system and do not store passwords.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at support@coreinsightgroup.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@coreinsightgroup.com.
